Chaos Mongers Team Helper

Cause we like to drink beer.

Battle Tactics

Ordained Charge (Chaos)

Pick an objective controlled by opponent.
Charge 2 friendly units.
Gain control of objective.

Offering of Carnage (Chaos)

Destroy 2 or more enemy units.

Seize the Center

Have 2 or more friendly units within 3" of the center of battlefield and not in combat.

Take the Flanks

Have 1 friendly unit within 6" of each short battlefield edge.
Units must not be wholly within friendly territory or set up this turn.

Take Their Land

Pick and control a terrain feature wholly or partially within enemy territory.
Must be wholly outside friendly territory.

Do Not Waver

2 or more friendly units fight and no friendly units are destroyed.

Slay the Entourage

Pick a unit in enemy general's regiment and destroy it.

Attack on Two Fronts

Control 2 or more objectives that you didn't control, and 1 of those was controlled by opponent.

Hero Phase

Abilities

Pledge to Chaos

  • Khorne: +1 melee attacks.
  • Nurgle: Ward (6+).
  • Slaanesh: 3d6 charge.
  • Tzeentch: (movement phase) Roll 2D6 then pick a point a number of inches equal to roll.
    Set up wholly within 6" of that point and more than 9" from enemy units.

Oracular Visions (Chaos Sorcerer)

Roll 3+: Unit wholly within 12" gets Ward (5+) until start of next turn.

Spells

Binding Damnation (Casting Value: 7)

Enemy unit within 12" has Strike-Last.

Daemonic Speed (Casting Value: 7)

Friendly unit wholly within 12" gets 3D6 to charge.

Spite-Tongue Curse (Casting Value: 5)

Inflict 3 mortal damage to enemy within 12".
If spell is unsuccessful or unbound, caster takes D3 mortal damage.
