COCOCAY

Ways to thrill

Cococay, Royal Caribbean's private island in the Bahamas, offers a treasure trove of thrilling activities for visitors of all ages. Adventure-seekers can soar to new heights on the island's exhilarating zip line, providing panoramic views of the crystal-clear waters and lush landscapes below. For those craving an adrenaline rush, the towering waterslides at Thrill Waterpark are sure to deliver, with heart-pounding twists and turns that leave you breathless. If underwater exploration is more your speed, don a snorkel and dive into the vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful marine life.

Ways to chill

Cococay invites visitors to unwind and rejuvenate amidst its tranquil surroundings, offering a myriad of relaxing options to soothe the soul. Sink your toes into the powdery white sands of Chill Island and bask in the warm Caribbean sun while gentle ocean breezes lull you into a state of utter serenity. Embark on a leisurely stroll along the shoreline, listening to the rhythmic sounds of the waves as they kiss the shore. For the ultimate relaxation experience, retreat to one of the island's private cabanas, where you can indulge in luxurious amenities and personalized service, all while gazing out at the endless expanse of turquoise waters.

Whether you're unwinding with a seaside massage, lounging in a hammock beneath swaying palm trees, or simply savoring the breathtaking beauty of nature, Cococay offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
