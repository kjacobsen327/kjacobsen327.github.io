Our Story

Bob wasn't your typical fisherman. He wasn't interested in giant catches or tall tales; his pride and joy was his boat, The Net Result, a quirky little vessel better suited for hauling snacks than sharks. Bob had a soft spot for the sea's tiniest treasures—anchovies, sardines, and shrimp.

One day, as Bob sorted his catch, a particularly feisty shrimp wriggled free and landed squarely on his sandwich. Bob chuckled, shook his head, and named the shrimp Scampi. “You've got spirit, kid,” he said. Inspired by Scampi's boldness, Bob hung up his nets and opened Lil Fishy, a tapas bar that puts small seafood in the spotlight.

- Bob's Family
